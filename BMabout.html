Our Vision

Our Founder, Amitkumar Upadhyay and Ashish Bhardwaaj founded Brainmentors Academy on 9th of November 2020. His motives are the words of Swami Vivekananda, “We want that education by which character is formed, strength of mind is increased, the intellect expanded, and by which one can stand on one’s own feet.”

We are now growing into an education institution which has positive influence over lives of many students and aspiring to benefit many more children.
